MENU CHANGE REQUESTS
Submit your requests at the following link: ubereats.typeform.com/to/UyWLlY
Error corrections are made within 48 hours
Minor changes (affecting less than 25% of the menu) are made within 3 working days
Major changes (affecting more than 25% of the menu) are made within 5 working days
New menu submissions have a lead time of 7 working days
